Blogwith Beta

Blogwith aims to integrate note taking applications to Wordpress. The goal is to allow users to publish content straight from their favourite note taking applications and not waste time switching between applications.

Why Blogwith?

If you write in Evernote, Simplenote or Onenote regularly and is looking for a service that allows you to publish straight to the different publishing platform, this is it. Think buffer for writing.
